Section 01

Data Governance & Non-Training Commitment

Ardak is designed with a privacy-first architecture.

  • No Training: Your prompts, queries, and the AI's responses are not used to train the underlying models.
  • Third-Party Processing: To provide high-performance LLM capabilities, Ardak utilizes Venice AI as a sub-processor.
No training Sub-processor: Venice AI Privacy-first

Section 02

Local Storage & User Responsibility

Unlike traditional cloud-based AI services, Ardak prioritizes local data sovereignty.

  • Local Records: Your entire chat history, messages, and Ardak's long-term memory (including notes regarding your profile and active projects) are stored locally on your device.
  • Data Portability & Risk: Because your data is stored locally, it is not synced across devices by our servers. If you switch devices or delete local files, the data cannot be recovered by Ardak.
Backup recommendation: To ensure data persistence, we highly recommend manually backing up your local data to a secure cloud provider (Google Drive, iCloud, OneDrive, Proton Drive).

Section 03

Registration & Authentication

Ardak does not maintain a proprietary registration or password database.

  • OAuth Integration: We use Continue with Google, Continue with GitHub, and Continue with Apple for secure authentication.
  • No Direct Credentials: We do not collect or store your passwords. Authentication is handled entirely by your chosen third-party provider.
Google GitHub Apple

Section 04

Identity Verification (KYC)

To ensure compliance with international financial regulations and enable the use of fiat currencies, all users must complete a Know Your Customer process.

  • Mandatory Requirements: Upload a valid Passport or National ID card, along with a clear selfie.
  • Verification Provider: Managed securely through Cryptomus.
  • Compliance: Otan Systems has successfully completed KYB (Know Your Business) verification.
