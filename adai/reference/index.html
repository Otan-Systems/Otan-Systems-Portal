Section 1
Application Purpose and Functionality

Adai is a decentralized, privacy-focused mapping tool designed to facilitate local and global Point of Interest (POI) management.

Local Processing
Offline Privacy
Core functionalities, including AI-driven natural language processing (EmbeddingGemma-300M) and speech-to-text (Vosk), operate entirely offline on your device to ensure maximum privacy.
Data Integrity
Zero Ecosystem Storage
The application does not generate or store user identification data within its own ecosystem. All global contributions are processed via OpenStreetMap (OSM) protocols.
🔑
Section 2
Global Contributions and Authentication
OSM Integration
OAuth 2.0 Auth
To contribute global POI data, you may choose to authenticate via OpenStreetMap using OAuth 2.0. This process ensures that you interact with the OSM database directly.
No User Account Storage
Local Tokens
Adai does not store your OSM credentials. The application only manages temporary authentication tokens locally to facilitate your contributions.
Data Responsibility: By submitting data to OSM, you acknowledge that your contributions become part of the OpenStreetMap project and are subject to their respective contributor terms.
🛡️
Section 3
Data Storage and Privacy
Local Data
Encrypted SQLite
All personal POI records marked as "Local" are stored exclusively in an encrypted SQLite database on your physical device.
Zero-Persistence Policy
Immediate Purge
Your search history and temporary session data are purged upon closing the application to ensure that no footprint of your activity remains.
Transparency: We do not collect, monetize, or track your geolocation data or habits.

Section 5
Limitation of Liability and Data Accuracy
AI Disclaimer
"As-Is" Tooling
While the AI engine (EmbeddingGemma) is designed to interpret natural language commands accurately, it is a tool provided "as-is". You are responsible for reviewing and confirming any POI details (the "Final Form") before they are saved locally or synchronized globally.
Conflicts
Resolution Mechanisms
The application provides mechanisms for resolving data conflicts between local and global POI sets during monthly updates. Adai is not responsible for the accuracy of global OSM data or discrepancies arising from external map updates.

Section 6
Updates and Modifications
Map Updates
Resumable Downloads
Map data (Geofabrik .osm.pbf) is updated periodically. The update process requires an active internet connection, and the application supports resumable downloads to minimize data disruption.
