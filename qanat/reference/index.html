Part 1
Privacy Policy

Qanat is built with privacy and minimalism at its core. The application runs entirely in your browser — nothing leaves your device.

Processing
100% Client-Side
Qanat operates entirely within your web browser. There are no backend servers processing your files.
Data Collection
None, ever
We do not upload, store, collect, or share your images, photo properties, or personal data.
Analytics
Zero Tracking
No intrusive tracking cookies or behavioral analytics. What you do on Qanat stays on your machine.
How a conversion flows
Step 1 · Local

You select an image

JPG, PNG, or JPEG is read directly from your device via the browser's File API.

Step 2 · In-Memory

Encoded in your browser

The file is converted to base64 in RAM using native browser APIs. No network call is made.

Step 3 · Output

You copy or download the result

The output stays local. Closing the tab erases it from memory.

What we do not collect
  • Your images or their content
  • EXIF or photo properties
  • Filenames or file sizes
  • IP addresses or geolocation
  • Device fingerprints
  • Behavioral or analytics events
  • Accounts, emails, or credentials
  • Cookies for tracking purposes
Because Qanat does not transmit your files anywhere, there is no server-side log capable of revealing what you converted. Privacy is enforced by architecture, not by promise.
